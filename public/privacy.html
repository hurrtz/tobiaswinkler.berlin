1. What this policy covers

This policy describes how SchnackAI processes personal data when you use the app. It covers the app itself and the data handling under my control as the app operator.

It does not replace the privacy policies of the AI, speech, or web-search providers you connect yourself. When you configure a provider in SchnackAI and send requests through it, that provider also processes data under its own privacy policy and terms.

2. Data processed by the app

SchnackAI may process the following categories of data, depending on how you use it:

  • audio you record when using voice input,
  • transcriptions created from that audio,
  • prompts, conversation history, and assistant replies,
  • provider configuration you choose, such as selected models, voices, and speech modes,
  • API keys you enter for third-party providers,
  • web-search queries and returned source links, if you enable web search,
  • optional local debug logs or exported conversation content, if you intentionally create or share them.

3. How SchnackAI stores data on your device

  • Conversation content and general app settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Provider API keys are stored separately in secure device storage.
  • The app and the operating system may create temporary local cache files for speech processing, playback, or exported logs. These files remain on the device unless you choose to share them or the app or operating system removes them.

SchnackAI does not require a SchnackAI user account for core functionality.

4. How data is sent to third-party providers

SchnackAI is designed around a bring-your-own-key model. When you configure an AI, speech-to-text, text-to-speech, or web-search provider, the app sends the relevant request directly to the provider you selected, using the API key you supplied.

This may include, depending on the feature you use:

  • audio for transcription,
  • text prompts and conversation context for AI responses,
  • text for speech synthesis,
  • search queries and related parameters for web search.

I do not operate a central SchnackAI backend that relays these core AI and speech requests on your behalf. The third-party provider you choose receives the data needed to fulfill your request.

5. Local and native speech features

Some speech features may run locally on the device or through native operating-system speech frameworks. In those cases, processing may happen on-device or through Apple system services, depending on the feature and platform capabilities.

If you switch to a provider-based speech mode, the selected provider will process the audio or text required for that request.

6. Sharing and disclosure

I do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be disclosed only in the following situations:

  • to the third-party providers you explicitly configure and use inside the app,
  • if you intentionally share conversation content or support logs yourself,
  • if disclosure is required by law or necessary to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

7. Analytics and tracking

Based on the current app architecture, SchnackAI does not use a dedicated analytics, advertising, or product-tracking SDK to profile users across apps or websites.

Apple, the App Store, or the third-party providers you use may process data under their own policies. Those third-party practices are outside this policy.

8. Legal bases under the GDPR

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, processing may rely on the following bases:

  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR, where processing is necessary to provide the functionality you request, such as voice input, AI replies, speech output, or provider connectivity.
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, where processing is necessary for legitimate interests such as app security, abuse prevention, debugging, and maintaining reliable operation.
  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR, where consent is relevant, for example where your device asks you for microphone permission.

9. Retention

  • Data stored locally in the app remains on your device until you delete it, remove the app, or overwrite it through normal usage.
  • API keys remain in secure device storage until you remove or replace them.
  • Temporary speech files and local logs may remain in device storage until removed by the app, the operating system, or by you.
  • Third-party providers may keep request data according to their own retention rules.

10. International transfers

The third-party providers you configure may process data in countries outside your own, including outside the European Economic Area. Before using a provider, you should review that provider’s privacy policy, security documentation, and regional processing terms.

11. Your rights

Depending on the law that applies to you, you may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because SchnackAI stores much of its operational data locally on your device and routes provider requests directly to the provider you choose, many deletion and access actions can be performed directly in the app, on your device, or with the relevant third-party provider.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions about SchnackAI, please contact contact@tobiaswinkler.berlin.
